About

An Online voting system that will replace the old balot paper system. Over the time we have utilized the required technology in every sector to improve efficiency and save the extra resources. But the votig systemis still very expensive and requires bigger workforce. The system is slower and still not completely tamper proof. We bring the system that is safe, reliable and solve the modern issues like higher reachability of the booth, the crowd free voting, inexpensive, faster results and others.

Feature

  • Secured by 256 bit encryption
  • Backed by ethereum based technology
  • Verifiable Transaction
  • Easy to use
  • Cheaper than balot voting system
  • Faster voting process
